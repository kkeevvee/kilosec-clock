I am thinking about a kilosecond clock, because seconds are the basic SI unit. So a full day (24 hrs) has 86 400 s = 86.4 ks.

It won't be really useful for day keeping in our society but it would be fun to have such a clock anyway.

00:01 am = 00.06 ks 08:00 am = 28.80 ks 12:00 pm = 43.20 ks 04:00 pm = 57.60 ks 10:00 pm = 79.20 ks etc.
